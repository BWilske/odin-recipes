poached eggs

Poached eggs are a breakfast time favorite of mine. theyre super easy when you get the hang of it, and when youre a mother of 12 the idea of just swirling the water and dumping everybodys food inside is very appealing The eggs should be shaped like a teardrop if cooked properly, and go great over toast with butter, hollandaise, or avocado.

items needed

  • eggs

instructions

  1. first set water on to boil
  2. then crack your eggs into a pouring vessel
  3. swirl the water and dump eggs in
  4. cook for 3 minutes and lightly towel off when done
