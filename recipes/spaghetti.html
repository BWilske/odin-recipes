Spaghetti

mama mia thassa spicy meatball. With my italian heritage i sure know how to cook a good spaghetti. unfortunately for you guys i dont really wanna share my secrets so instead well be learning to make spaghetti from a can. spaghetti is a great dish if youre the average american who must consume at least 10 percent of their bodyweight in easily processed carbs per day. This will get you at least halfway there and the noodles are super easy so you can make this dish even if youre lazy!

  • spaghetti
  • herbs
  • canned sauce
  • some sort of meat or meatballs
  1. first put your water on to boil
  2. then put your sauce on to heat up
  3. spice the sauce using your choice of herbs
  4. place your spaghetti in the pot to cook
  5. add your choice of meat to the sauce
  6. pull the spaghetti out of the water and strain it
  7. add sauce and enjoy, perhaps top with cheese
