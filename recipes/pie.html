PIE

pie is a very yummy dish that can be filled with many different toppings. My favorite is apple pie because that is ehat my grandmother used to make. although she swore me to the secrecy of her pie recipe on her deathbed, here i am to share it with all you strangers on the internet! enjoy

items needed

  • flour
  • butter
  • salt
  • pie filling

instructions

  1. first combine dry ingredients
  2. then roll the pie crust out
  3. fill with your choice of ingredients
  4. bake at 400 for 45 minutes
